# Glossary

## Admin

A platform operator who can review verifications, refunds, payouts, reports, and audit records.

## Campaign

A fundraising page with a story, goal, lifecycle state, updates, and donation records.

## CSR

Corporate Social Responsibility. In this app, CSR covers corporate campaigns, employee attribution, match pledges, and settlement flows.

## Demo Payment

A non-production payment record used for demos. It must not count as real public impact.

## Form 10BD

An Indian compliance filing related to donation reporting by eligible organizations.

## Form 10BE

An official certificate document for eligible donations. It is not the same as an app-generated receipt.

## NGO

Non-governmental organization. In DaanSetu, an NGO can own a public profile, campaigns, volunteer opportunities, payout settings, and compliance documents.

## Paise

Smallest common unit of INR. DaanSetu stores application money amounts as integer paise.

## RLS

Row Level Security. Supabase PostgreSQL policy system that limits which rows users can read or write.

## Supporter

A normal user who donates, volunteers, follows, bookmarks, posts, or participates in public platform activity.

## Verification

The admin-reviewed process that gives an NGO trusted status.
